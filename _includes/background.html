Background

I’m a Computer Science student at the University of Alberta with a passion for building high-quality software across data, web, and game development. I’ve worked as a Data Analyst Intern at Chase IT Consulting, where I built Power BI dashboards, automated validation pipelines, and supported large-scale data and ETL workflows. I’ve also contributed to full-stack projects and helped build end-to-end web experiences, including launching Forteguard.ca and maintaining multiple WordPress sites.

Beyond analytics, I enjoy engineering interactive and scalable software. I’ve built a complete 2D platformer in Unreal Engine 5, developed a distributed real-time collaboration app using Python, Flask, and WebSockets, and actively contribute to the UofA Autonomous Robotic Vehicle Project by improving ROS2 modules, documentation, and developer tools.

Across all my work, I focus on creating systems that feel smooth, intuitive, and performant — whether it’s a clean dashboard, a responsive web app, or a fast gameplay mechanic. My goal is always the same: build reliable, well-designed software that feels great to use.

When I'm not in front of a computer screen, I'm probably at the gym, cooking, spending time with my friends and family, or enjoying a good book.
